About Us

Services and approach provided by Owl Voice Swapper

This system will provide you with the capacity to convert audio from one language to another. For the time being, it's only a prototype of our concept, in which the user goes to our website and uploads a wave file that he wants to translate into another language. The audio will then be detected and converted to text using our technology. After that, the text is translated into English and delivered to the other cloning function. Before creating new audio in the same person's voice with identical qualities, this function requires 5 seconds of audio and text.

Another feature of this website is that users may develop AI bots for their desired voices by providing 5 seconds of audio and then passing the text they want the bot to recite. The AI bot will then begin speaking that text after some processing.

Both functions result in an audio file that the user may listen or download from the web page, and if the user wants to translate any text into Urdu, they can do it with only one click.
